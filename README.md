# 📊 Customer Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a **Customer Analysis Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze customer behavior, sales performance, and regional distribution.

The dashboard provides insights into customer segments, top customers, and sales distribution to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze total customers and overall sales performance  
- Identify top customers contributing to revenue  
- Understand customer distribution across segments and regions  
- Evaluate regional sales performance  
- Enable interactive filtering for deeper analysis  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- 👥 Total Customers: **793**  
- 💰 Total Sales: **2.26M**  
- 📈 Avg Sales per Customer: **2.85K**  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Modeling  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- 🔍 Interactive filters (Region, Segment, Customer Name)  
- 📊 KPI cards for quick performance overview  
- 📌 Customer distribution by segment (Bar + Pie chart)  
- 🌍 Customer & sales distribution by region  
- 🏆 Top customers based on sales contribution  
- 🎯 Clean and structured layout for easy insights

## 📊 Dashboard Breakdown

### 🔝 Top Section (KPIs)

### 📊 Middle Section
- **Customer Distribution by Segment**

---

### 📉 Bottom Section
- **Sales by Region**
  - West region leads in sales  
  - South region shows comparatively lower performance  

- **Customer Segment Distribution (Pie Chart)**
  - Consumer: ~52% (largest share)  
  - Corporate: ~30%  
  - Home Office: ~18%  

- **Filters**
  - Region filter (Central, East, South, West)  
  - Segment filter (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)  
  - Customer-level filtering for detailed analysis  

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- 📈 Consumer segment contributes the highest number of customers and sales  
- 🏆 A small group of customers drives a significant portion of total revenue  
- 🌍 Sales and customer distribution are concentrated in West and East regions  
- 📉 South region shows relatively lower customer count and sales
